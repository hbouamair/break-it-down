About BREAK IT DOWN

The dance adventure is called "Break It Down" and it is an innovative journey that fuses breakdancing with various dance styles from around the world, while spreading a message of love and peace. The concept behind the adventure is to showcase the beauty and diversity of dance from different cultures, while using the power of movement to break down cultural barriers and promote understanding and unity.

the Creator of the concept

Smarty is a highly skilled and respected bboy, known for his incredible footwork, dynamic power moves, and creative style. He has been dancing for over a decade and has won numerous competitions and battles locally
